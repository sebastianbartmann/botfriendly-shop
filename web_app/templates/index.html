Why Optimize for AI?

Traditional search is evolving into AI-assisted shopping. Agents like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity actively browse the web to find and recommend products. If your site isn't technically prepared, AI bots will skip your products entirely.

How the Scan Works

We simulate the behavior of modern AI agents. We analyze your robots.txt, look for machine-readable discovery files, validate your structured JSON-LD data, and check if your HTML semantics enable accurate extraction.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Optimization (AIO)?
It's the process of structuring your website so that Large Language Models (LLMs) and AI agents can accurately read, understand, and recommend your content or products to end-users.
Is this different from traditional SEO?
Yes. While SEO focuses on ranking pages for human searchers on Google, AIO focuses on providing perfect, structured, machine-readable facts (like exact pricing, stock, and specs) directly to an AI agent without relying on visual layouts.
Why did my site get a low score?
You may be accidentally blocking AI bots in your robots.txt, lacking a machine-readable llms.txt file, or having mismatched pricing data between your visible HTML and your backend JSON-LD schema.
